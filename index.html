Options

  • Full window: 
    <div class='slider full-window'>
  • Full screen: 
    <div class='slider full-screen'>
  • Slide duration 
    <div class='slider' data-duration=1>
    in seconds. Default is .2.
  • Vertically center slides' content with 
    <div class='slider align-middle'>
  • Detach slider nav by adding an id to the slider and creating 
    <div class='slider-nav' data-for='slider-id'>
    elsewhere
  • URI with hash matching a slide id in a slider, auto slides to it.
  • data-peek=20%
    to peek at neighboring slides, where 20% becomes 20% padding on left and right
